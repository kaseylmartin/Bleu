About Me

Bleu Valerio is an intimacy coordinator, psychotherapist, movement artist, and doula dedicated to fostering safe, consent-driven, and creatively enriching spaces for intimate storytelling. With a background in mental health, body awareness, and advocacy, Bleu brings a trauma-informed and body-conscious approach to every project, ensuring that actors feel empowered in their boundaries while supporting directors in realizing their creative vision.

As a psychotherapist specializing in sexual trauma, Bleu understands the complexities of navigating vulnerability in performance. This expertise allows them to create spaces that honor personal boundaries while facilitating authentic, intentional storytelling. Drawing from years of experience in movement artistry, Bleu also choreographs intimate scenes with precision and care, ensuring they are both emotionally and physically safe for performers.

Their work is particularly focused on centering the narratives of BIPOC and transgender individuals, with a commitment to representation, liberation, and transformative storytelling. As a certified intimacy professional (in progress), Bleu believes that intimacy coordination is not just about safety—it’s about fostering trust, authenticity, and the freedom to tell bold, nuanced stories.

Whether on set or in rehearsal spaces, Bleu’s goal is to create environments where both artistry and well-being thrive.

Let’s collaborate to bring your story to life with care, intention, and creativity.
