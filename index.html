About Me

Hi, I'm Bleu Valerio, an intimacy coordinator dedicated to creating safe, respectful, and authentic performances on set. With a focus on fostering a space of trust, I collaborate with actors and directors to navigate intimate scenes with care, ensuring emotional and physical safety for all involved.

My work is rooted in a deep understanding of boundaries, empathy, and communication. I provide guidance to actors during intimate scenes, ensuring their comfort while respecting their personal limits. With years of experience and a passion for creating meaningful, respectful performances, I strive to make every project a safe, inclusive space for all talent involved.

I believe in the importance of clear communication and mutual respect in the creative process. My role as an intimacy coordinator is not only about facilitating physical scenes but also ensuring that every actor feels seen, heard, and valued in every moment of their performance.
